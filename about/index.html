Too shrewd to be naïve too brute to be fine.



After seventeen semesters in art-school Lee finally recieved a BFA then spent a decade VJing (with Tsuyoshi Suzuki, Orbital, Aphex Twin, and GodSpeed You! Black Emperor) and making experimental documentaries about climate-change solutions. He has won awards for his paintings (Laureat du Quebec; CollegeArt '86), videos (Canadian Student Film Festival), and climate movies (EarthVision'98 in Santa Cruz). The Space In Between, Lee's paintings drawings and video chaos have recently been written about in "Tripping on nothing: placebo psychedelics and contextual factors" (published in Springer Nature 2020) where they say his paintings make people as high as if they ate mushrooms.
